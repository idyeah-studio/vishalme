About

Designer. Engineer.
Human who has shipped both.

I started as a software engineer. That decision shaped everything.

Most designers hand off to engineering. I sit at the seam — where design decisions meet implementation reality, where a component architecture choice determines whether a product team moves fast or grinds to a halt. That's where I've spent 25 years.

At Walmart I architected the DV Design System and built the component foundation for Luminate Insights — a high-density analytics platform where merchants make multi-million dollar inventory decisions. At Apple's Wallet, Payments, and Commerce team I've been driving an AI-augmented workflow that takes features from brief to production-ready React in under two weeks, at Apple-standard quality.
